Porter Perkins

Published: Thu 20 April 2023
Updated: Wed 27 September 2023

DRAFT

Several publications by Porter Perkins were included in
The Historical Selected Bibliography of NACA-NASA Icing Publications in the "Meteorology of Icing Clouds" section. While the publications are important and useful, they did not directly affect the icing design values adopted in regulations. I will review those in their own thread, to be published at a later date.
Perkins was quite productive during his career at NACA and NASA.
